Library Rules & Regulations

1. Membership

All students and faculty must register to borrow books. Library cards are non-transferable.

2. Borrowing Limits
  • Students may borrow up to 3 books at a time.
  • Faculty may borrow up to 5 books.
3. Loan Period & Fines
  • Standard loan period is 14 days.
  • Overdue fines are charged at ₹2.00 per day.
  • Lost books must be replaced or paid for at market value.
4. General Conduct

Silence must be maintained. No food or drink allowed inside the library premises.
